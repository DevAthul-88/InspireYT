Privacy Policy
==============

Introduction
------------

Welcome to InspireYT! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our AI tool for generating tags, scripts, ideas, titles, trending video suggestions, descriptions, and more for YouTube content creators.

Information We Collect
----------------------

We collect the following types of information when you use our tool:

### 1\. **Personal Information**

When you sign up or use our service, we may collect personal information such as:

-   **Email Address**: Required to create an account and communicate important updates.
-   **Username**: Used to identify your account and personalize your experience.

### 2\. **Content Data**

Any content you input into our tool (e.g., video ideas, titles, or descriptions) is processed to provide you with relevant outputs and recommendations.

### 3\. **Usage Data**

We collect information on how you interact with our tool, including:

-   Log data such as the date and time of access, features used, and clicks.
-   Device information, such as IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use the Information
--------------------------

We use your information to:

1.  Provide and improve our services.
2.  Personalize your experience with relevant recommendations and outputs.
3.  Communicate with you about updates, new features, and support.
4.  Analyze usage trends to enhance the tool's performance and functionality.

Data Sharing and Disclosure
---------------------------

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

1.  **Service Providers**: We may share information with third-party service providers who help us operate our tool (e.g., hosting, analytics, and customer support) but are bound by confidentiality agreements.
2.  **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Data Security
-------------

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Your Choices and Rights
-----------------------

You have the following rights regarding your data:

1.  **Access and Update**: You can access and update your personal information through your account settings.
2.  **Delete Account**: You can delete your account at any time, which will remove all associated personal information.
3.  **Opt-Out**: You can opt out of promotional communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies
---------------------------------

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and understand user preferences. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy
----------------------

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and if significant changes are made, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website.

Contact Us
----------

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, feel free to reach out to us at devathulvinod@gmail.com.
